Organisers

FOSSEE (Free and Open Source Software in Education) project promotes the use of FOSS tools to improve the quality of education in our country. We aim to reduce dependency on proprietary software in educational institutions. We encourage the use of FOSS tools through various activities to ensure commercial software is replaced by equivalent FOSS tools. We also develop new FOSS tools and upgrade existing tools to meet requirements in academia and research. The FOSSEE project is part of the National Mission on Education through Information and Communication Technology (ICT), Ministry of Human Resources and Development, Government of India.

FAQ

1. Do we have to pay to attend this meetup?

No. This meetup is absolutely free.

2. Is this meet up open for women working as Academic Faculty or Non-Profits?

Yes. This workshop is open to all women irrespective of their professions. Women from Academia, Industry, Non-Profit sector, Students, Teachers and Faculty are welcome to attend.

3. Do you provide stay/accommodation and lunch/dinner?

No, This is a free 1 day meetup run by volunteers and hence, unfortunately, we are unable to provide accommodation or lunch/dinner

4. Is there any age limit to the participants?

No. Women of all ages can participate.

5. Can men attend this meetup?

The meetup is geared towards women in STEM. We do encourage attendees with +1 gentlemen guests to attend.
